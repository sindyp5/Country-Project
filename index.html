Panama

On this page you'll see a snippet of what Panama has to provide in cuisine + other stuff

Panama, known for the famous Panamal Canal is located by Central America. It's basically right between Mexico and South America if you were to find it on a map. It's got a bit of everything, mountains, coastal lowlands, and archipelagos. On the right, you can see a famous statue in Panama, called the "Balboa Statue", it was gifted by the Spanish.
