Panama's Geography

Panama is found right on the narrow bridge of land connnecting North America and South America. Panama has over 1000 islands off of the Atlantic and Pacific coast.


Panama Canal

Panama has over 1000 islands off of the Atlantic and Pacific coast. This country is mainly known for the Panama Canal as nearly 800,0000 people have visited the Canal just this year.

Sancocho: Panama's National Dish

Sancocho is very well known as well as considered to be Panama's national dish. This dish originated in the Azuero Peninsula of Southwestern Panama, the ingredients for sancocho was a mix from the "Old World" and the "New World":

- Hen

- Yam

- Culantro

- Water

- Salt

Note: People often add spices of their own choice
